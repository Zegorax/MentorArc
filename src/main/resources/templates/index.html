MentorArc connects students (poulain) and teachers (mentor) to help each other.

As an admin, you have access to the Admin page

As a poulain, you can see all the proposals for help made by mentors on the All proposals page. However, if none of the mentor or schedule suits you, you can always create a request for help, indicating subject, schedule and comment. All mentors will see your request and will be able to suggest themselves if they are available and able to help you.

As a poulain, you can see all your proposals and all your requests for help.

As a mentor, you can see all requests for help made by poulains on the All Requests page. Feel free to browse around and offer yourself if you are available. If you want to show your availability at a certain time in a certain subject, you can always create a help proposal. This will let the poulains know where and when to find you.

As a mentor, you can see all your help proposals and all your requests for help.
